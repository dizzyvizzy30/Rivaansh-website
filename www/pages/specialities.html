Our Specialities

At Rivaansh ENT Clinic, we offer a wide range of specialized services to address all your ear, nose, and throat needs. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing the highest quality care in a variety of sub-specialties.

Pediatric ENT

We provide specialized care for children with ENT conditions, including ear infections, tonsillitis, and breathing problems.

Rhinology

Our rhinology specialists focus on the treatment of nasal and sinus disorders, including chronic sinusitis, nasal polyps, and allergies.

Laryngology

We offer advanced diagnosis and treatment for voice and swallowing disorders, including vocal cord paralysis, polyps, and nodules.

Otology & Neurotology

Our otology experts specialize in the treatment of ear and hearing disorders, including hearing loss, tinnitus, and vertigo.

Head & Neck Surgery

We provide comprehensive care for patients with head and neck cancers, including tumors of the mouth, throat, and larynx.
